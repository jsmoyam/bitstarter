FAQ

What is the process to solve my problem?
Simple. Write your problem. Wait for offers. Choose an offer. You can rank the offer and submit your opinion.
Is there any cost?
No! We connect persons with you to solve your needs. You only pay to people if you accept their offers.
Can I write any problem I have??
Of course! You can write and explain whatever problem you have. People will read it and you will start receiving offers.
